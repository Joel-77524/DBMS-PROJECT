The Conjuring (2013)

Horror / Mystery / Thriller

(4.5 / 5 - Critics)

Synopsis

Based on the true case files of paranormal investigators Ed and Lorraine Warren. The film tells the terrifying story of the Perron family, who move into an isolated farmhouse in Rhode Island and soon discover they are sharing the space with a dark, demonic entity. To save the family from a malicious haunting, the Warrens must confront one of the most frightening supernatural cases of their careers.

Director:

James Wan

Cast:

Vera Farmiga, Patrick Wilson, Lili Taylor

Running Time:

112 minutes

Release Date:

July 19, 2013

Audience Reviews

Horror Fanatic

One of the best haunted house movies of the last decade. It relies on atmosphere and slow-burn terror rather than cheap jump scares. The performances by Farmiga and Wilson as the Warrens are flawless. A must-watch for anyone who loves genuinely terrifying cinema.
